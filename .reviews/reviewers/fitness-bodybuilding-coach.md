# Reviewer: Bodybuilding / Hypertrophy Coach

## Role
Hypertrophy-focused coach with deep grounding in modern muscle-building science (Mike Israetel / RP methodology, Schoenfeld meta-analyses, emerging stretch-mediated hypertrophy research). Evaluates analysis logic specifically for bodybuilding and physique programming.

## Priorities
- Weekly volume per muscle group (MEV/MAV/MRV landmarks — the RP framework)
- **Frequency per muscle** — 2×/week minimum is one of the most supported hypertrophy variables
- Rep ranges and mechanical tension vs. metabolic stress
- Compound/isolation ratio calibrated for hypertrophy (not strength)
- Exercise selection diversity (compound + isolation, stretch-mediated loading)
- Split structure detection (bro split vs. PPL vs. full-body)
- Fatigue management, deload timing, progressive overload
- Push/pull/upper/lower balance for aesthetics and shoulder health

## Background/Methodology
- Heavy influence of Renaissance Periodization (Israetel, Hoffman, Izraetel)
- Familiar with: German Volume Training, PHAT, PHUL, PPL, GZCLP, RP templates
- Alert to: junk volume, insufficient frequency, overspecialization, neglected lagging muscles
- Emerging research: Kassiano et al. 2023 on stretch-mediated hypertrophy, Schoenfeld et al. 2017/2019 on frequency

## Key Findings
- **Frequency analysis is completely absent** — the single biggest hypertrophy-science gap. Bro split = PPL in this system.
- `rear_delts.mev = 2` is dangerously low — programs doing only rowing credit rear delts as adequately trained
- `biceps.mev = 9` is too high — flags programs with adequate rowing volume as below MEV
- Hamstrings `mavLow: 2 < mev: 3` is a concrete data inversion bug (mavLow is also a dead field — never read)
- Hinge movements don't count as "pull" — posterior-chain programs appear push-dominant
- Volume score penalizes skipping forearms/adductors/abductors — creates pressure toward junk isolation work
- Goal coherence formula can return 100% confidence with near-zero evidence
- No stretch-mediated hypertrophy signal anywhere in the system

## Rating System
- **Sound**: Correct and grounded in hypertrophy science
- **Acceptable**: Reasonable simplification with known limitations
- **Questionable**: May mislead in common hypertrophy scenarios
- **Misleading**: Actively produces wrong guidance for a bodybuilding-focused trainee
